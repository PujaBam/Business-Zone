Step-by-step business setup in Dubai

Setting up a business in Dubai can be a complex and daunting process. As your partner, Business Incorporation Zone can save you time, money and worry. Here’s a simple overview of the process for setting up a business in Dubai:

1. Prepare your documents

You’ll need to ensure you have your paperwork in order to set up your business in Dubai, from passport copies to no-objection letters.

2. Purchase a business license

To operate your business, you need to have the right license. There are a few options for you to choose from: service, trading or commercial.

3. Arrange your working visa

Businesses require working visas for legal residence. You’ll need to ensure you have the correct entry working permits for your employees.
