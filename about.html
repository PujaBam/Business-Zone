Our Story

Our company was established back in 2009 to provide what we knew was missing from the market: a higher level of professional care and support during business setup in Dubai, UAE. In the years since, we have grown through close partnerships with Fujairah Creative City Media Free Zone and Dubai Department of Economic Development to become the premier business setup provider in the UAE. We’re proud of our background of building trust through integrity (and we’re not above shouting about it).
