    How to set up your business in DMCC

    DMCC company setup is fast, easy and convenient. Here’s a simple overview of the process:

    Prepare your documents

    You’ll need to ensure you have your paperwork in order to set up your business in Dubai, from passport copies to no-objection letters.

    Purchase a business license

    To operate your business, you need to have the right license. There are a few options for you to choose from in DMCC: Trading, Service and Industrial.

    Arrange your working visa

    Businesses require working visas for legal residence. You’ll need to ensure you have the correct entry working permits for your employees.

    Set up your bank account

    Setting up a corporate bank account in the UAE can be a confusing process but at Business Incorporation Zone we can help you to select, apply and open one that ticks every box.
